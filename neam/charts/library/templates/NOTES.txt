Thank you for installing the NEAM Platform Helm Library Chart.

This library chart provides reusable Kubernetes resource templates for all NEAM Platform microservices.

To use this library in your own charts, add it as a dependency in your Chart.yaml:

  dependencies:
    - name: library
      version: "1.0.0"
      repository: "file://../library"

Then use the provided templates in your chart's templates directory:

  {{- include "library.deployment" . -}}
  {{- include "library.service" . -}}
  {{- include "library.ingress" . -}}
  {{- include "library.configmap" . -}}
  {{- include "library.serviceAccount" . -}}
  {{- include "library.horizontalPodAutoscaler" . -}}
  {{- include "library.podDisruptionBudget" . -}}
  {{- include "library.persistentVolumeClaim" . -}}

For detailed documentation, see the README.md file.
